## 🎤 Q&A Prep (Most Likely Questions)

| Question | One-Line Answer |
|---|---|
| "How do you identify at-risk students if it's anonymous?" | "We track patterns, not people. Same device = same pattern. No name ever." |
| "How do you know confusion actually decreased?" | "The trend line in our session history. We show you the graph, not just the claim." |
| "What stops a student from spamming signals?" | "60-second cooldown per device. One signal per minute maximum." |
| "Why wouldn't a teacher just ask the class verbally?" | "Because 72% of confused students stay silent even when directly asked." |
| "What's your unfair advantage vs Google Classroom?" | "Google Classroom sends assignments. We tell you which concept is failing in real-time before the test." |
