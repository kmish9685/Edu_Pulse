## ✅ What EduPulse Specifically Improves

### For Students
- Silent confusion → flagged and addressed within minutes, not weeks
- Anonymous voice in every classroom — no fear, no embarrassment
- Personalized AI review material sent after every session

### For Teachers
- Real-time confusion signal dashboard during class
- AI drafts the follow-up email and quiz automatically — zero extra work
- Session-over-session data to see if their teaching is landing

### For Institutions
- Early warning system flags at-risk students 3–4 weeks before dropout
- Every signal is logged → builds a confusion data library per course
- Reduces dropout-related revenue loss of **$21,000 USD per student**
